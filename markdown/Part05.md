## 201. money

- CEFR: A1
- POS: noun
- IPA (UK): /ˈmʌni/
- Chinese: 钱；货币；财富
- Collocation: make money;save money;spend money
- Example: I never carry much money in cash.
- 例句: 我从不随身带很多现金。
- Synonym: cash;funds;currency
- MemoryTip: 不可数；coins/notes 才可数。
