# English Core 3000 · Part01

Rank 1–50（the … see），共 50 词。

## 1. the

- CEFR: A1
- POS: det.; art.
- IPA (UK): /ðə/
- Chinese: 这；那（定冠词）
- Collocation: the same;the first;the more
- Example: The book on the table is mine.
- 例句: 桌上的那本书是我的。
- MemoryTip: 元音前常读 /ðiː/，辅音前读 /ðə/。

## 2. be

- CEFR: A1
- POS: verb
- IPA (UK): /biː/
- Chinese: 是；在；成为
- Collocation: be careful;be honest;there be
- Example: She wants to be a doctor one day.
- 例句: 她希望有一天成为医生。
- Synonym: exist;remain
- MemoryTip: am/is/are/was/were 都是 be 的词形。

## 3. and

- CEFR: A1
- POS: conj.
- IPA (UK): /ænd/
- Chinese: 和；并且
- Collocation: and so on;bread and butter;and then
- Example: Tom and Lily walked home together.
- 例句: 汤姆和莉莉一起走回家。
- Synonym: plus;also
- Antonym: but;or
- MemoryTip: 连接对等成分，可记成“一加一”。

## 4. of

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.
- IPA (UK): /əv/
- Chinese: ……的；属于
- Collocation: a lot of;kind of;proud of
- Example: This is a photo of my family.
- 例句: 这是我家的一张照片。
- Synonym: from;about
- MemoryTip: 弱读常见 /əv/，别总读成 /ɒv/。

## 5. to

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; particle
- IPA (UK): /tuː/
- Chinese: 到；向；去；不定式标记
- Collocation: go to;listen to;want to
- Example: I need to finish my homework tonight.
- 例句: 我今晚需要完成作业。
- Synonym: towards;for
- Antonym: from
- MemoryTip: 作不定式时多弱读 /tə/。

## 6. a

- CEFR: A1
- POS: det.; art.
- IPA (UK): /ə/
- Chinese: 一个（不定冠词）
- Collocation: a bit;a few;have a look
- Example: She bought a gift for her friend.
- 例句: 她给朋友买了一份礼物。
- Synonym: one;any
- Antonym: the
- MemoryTip: 辅音前用 a，元音前改用 an。

## 7. in

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /ɪn/
- Chinese: 在……里；在……时
- Collocation: in time;in fact;in the morning
- Example: There are three books in the bag.
- 例句: 包里有三本书。
- Synonym: inside;within
- Antonym: out;outside
- MemoryTip: in 像走进门里。

## 8. have

- CEFR: A1
- POS: verb
- IPA (UK): /hæv/
- Chinese: 有；吃；经历；助动词
- Collocation: have lunch;have to;have fun
- Example: They have a small flat near the station.
- 例句: 他们在车站附近有一套小公寓。
- Synonym: own;possess
- Antonym: lack
- MemoryTip: 完成时里 have/has + 过去分词。

## 9. it

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /ɪt/
- Chinese: 它；这；形式主语
- Collocation: it seems;find it;make it
- Example: It is raining hard this afternoon.
- 例句: 今天下午雨下得很大。
- Synonym: this;that
- MemoryTip: 指物、动物、时间天气常用 it。

## 10. you

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /juː/
- Chinese: 你；你们
- Collocation: thank you;see you;you know
- Example: Can you help me with this box?
- 例句: 你能帮我搬这个箱子吗？
- Synonym: one;people
- MemoryTip: 单复数同形，语气看语境。

## 11. he

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /hiː/
- Chinese: 他
- Collocation: he says;he is;there he is
- Example: He is waiting for the bus outside.
- 例句: 他在外面等公交车。
- Antonym: she
- MemoryTip: 主格 he，宾格 him，所有格 his。

## 12. for

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; conj.
- IPA (UK): /fɔː(r)/
- Chinese: 为了；给；因为；达……之久
- Collocation: for example;look for;wait for
- Example: This gift is for your sister.
- 例句: 这份礼物是给你妹妹的。
- Synonym: because of;during
- Antonym: against
- MemoryTip: 表目的、对象或时间长度都很常见。

## 13. they

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /ðeɪ/
- Chinese: 他们；她们；它们
- Collocation: they say;there they are;as they
- Example: They arrived early for the meeting.
- 例句: 他们早早到了会议现场。
- Synonym: people
- MemoryTip: 主格 they，宾格 them，所有格 their。

## 14. not

- CEFR: A1
- POS: adv.; particle
- IPA (UK): /nɒt/
- Chinese: 不；没有
- Collocation: not only;do not;not yet
- Example: I do not want to go out tonight.
- 例句: 我今晚不想出门。
- Synonym: no;never
- Antonym: yes
- MemoryTip: 常缩成 n't，如 don't / isn't。

## 15. that

- CEFR: A1
- POS: det.; pron.; conj.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /ðæt/
- Chinese: 那个；那；引导从句
- Collocation: so that;that day;find that
- Example: I think that the answer is correct.
- 例句: 我认为这个答案是正确的。
- Synonym: which;this
- Antonym: this
- MemoryTip: 可指物，也可引导 that 从句。

## 16. we

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /wiː/
- Chinese: 我们
- Collocation: we are;as we;shall we
- Example: We are planning a trip next month.
- 例句: 我们计划下个月去旅行。
- Synonym: us
- MemoryTip: 主格 we，宾格 us，所有格 our。

## 17. on

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /ɒn/
- Chinese: 在……上；关于；继续
- Collocation: on time;on the table;turn on
- Example: The keys are on the kitchen table.
- 例句: 钥匙在厨房桌上。
- Synonym: upon;about
- Antonym: off;under
- MemoryTip: 接触表面用 on，反义常想 off。

## 18. with

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.
- IPA (UK): /wɪð/
- Chinese: 和……一起；用；带有
- Collocation: with care;deal with;along with
- Example: She went to the cinema with her friends.
- 例句: 她和朋友们一起去看电影了。
- Synonym: alongside;using
- Antonym: without
- MemoryTip: with 的反义是 without。

## 19. this

- CEFR: A1
- POS: det.; pron.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /ðɪs/
- Chinese: 这；这个
- Collocation: this morning;this way;like this
- Example: This café makes excellent coffee.
- 例句: 这家咖啡馆咖啡很好。
- Synonym: these;that
- Antonym: that
- MemoryTip: 近处 this，远处 that；复数 these。

## 20. i

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /aɪ/
- Chinese: 我
- Collocation: I think;I am;I hope
- Example: I usually get up at seven.
- 例句: 我通常七点起床。
- Synonym: me
- MemoryTip: 句子开头须大写 I。

## 21. do

- CEFR: A1
- POS: verb; aux.
- IPA (UK): /duː/
- Chinese: 做；干；助动词
- Collocation: do homework;do well;how do you
- Example: What do you usually do at weekends?
- 例句: 你周末通常做什么？
- Synonym: perform;act
- MemoryTip: 疑问/否定句里常用 do/does/did。

## 22. as

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; conj.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /æz/
- Chinese: 作为；当……时；像；因为
- Collocation: as well;as soon as;as for
- Example: She works as a nurse in hospital.
- 例句: 她在医院当护士。
- Synonym: like;while
- MemoryTip: as…as 表示同等比较。

## 23. at

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.
- IPA (UK): /æt/
- Chinese: 在（地点/时间点）；朝向
- Collocation: at home;at least;look at
- Example: We will meet at the station at six.
- 例句: 我们六点在车站见面。
- Synonym: in;on
- MemoryTip: 具体时刻、较小地点常用 at。

## 24. she

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.
- IPA (UK): /ʃiː/
- Chinese: 她
- Collocation: she says;she is;there she is
- Example: She teaches English at our school.
- 例句: 她在我们学校教英语。
- Antonym: he
- MemoryTip: 主格 she，宾格 her，所有格 her。

## 25. but

- CEFR: A1
- POS: conj.; prep.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /bʌt/
- Chinese: 但是；除了
- Collocation: but also;nothing but;last but not least
- Example: I like tea, but I prefer coffee.
- 例句: 我喜欢茶，但更爱咖啡。
- Synonym: however;yet
- Antonym: and
- MemoryTip: 转折用 but，语气比 however 更口语。

## 26. from

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.
- IPA (UK): /frɒm/
- Chinese: 从；来自；由于
- Collocation: from home;far from;different from
- Example: This letter is from my uncle in London.
- 例句: 这封信来自我在伦敦的叔叔。
- Synonym: out of;since
- Antonym: to;towards
- MemoryTip: from A to B：从 A 到 B。

## 27. by

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /baɪ/
- Chinese: 被；通过；在旁边；到……之前
- Collocation: by bus;by chance;by the way
- Example: The report was written by my colleague.
- 例句: 这份报告是我同事写的。
- Synonym: via;near
- MemoryTip: 被动语态里 by 引出动作执行者。

## 28. will

- CEFR: A1
- POS: modal; noun
- IPA (UK): /wɪl/
- Chinese: 将；会；意愿；遗嘱
- Collocation: will be;free will;if you will
- Example: They will call you tomorrow morning.
- 例句: 他们明天早上会给你打电话。
- Synonym: shall;going to
- Antonym: won't
- MemoryTip: 表将来也表意愿；won't = will not。

## 29. or

- CEFR: A1
- POS: conj.
- IPA (UK): /ɔː(r)/
- Chinese: 或者；否则
- Collocation: or so;either or;or else
- Example: Would you like tea or coffee?
- 例句: 你想喝茶还是咖啡？
- Synonym: alternatively
- Antonym: and
- MemoryTip: either…or… 表示二选一。

## 30. say

- CEFR: A1
- POS: verb
- IPA (UK): /seɪ/
- Chinese: 说；表示
- Collocation: say hello;say sorry;that is to say
- Example: Please say your name clearly.
- 例句: 请清楚地说出你的名字。
- Synonym: speak;tell;state
- Antonym: listen
- MemoryTip: say 侧重说的内容；tell 常带对象。

## 31. go

- CEFR: A1
- POS: verb
- IPA (UK): /ɡəʊ/
- Chinese: 去；走；变得
- Collocation: go home;go on;go shopping
- Example: We usually go to school by bike.
- 例句: 我们通常骑自行车去上学。
- Synonym: leave;travel;move
- Antonym: stay;come
- MemoryTip: go + -ing：go swimming / go shopping。

## 32. so

- CEFR: A1
- POS: adv.; conj.
- IPA (UK): /səʊ/
- Chinese: 如此；所以；这样
- Collocation: so that;so far;and so on
- Example: It was raining, so we stayed inside.
- 例句: 下雨了，所以我们待在屋里。
- Synonym: therefore;very
- MemoryTip: 既表程度（so tired），也表结果。

## 33. all

- CEFR: A1
- POS: det.; pron.; adv.
- IPA (UK): /ɔːl/
- Chinese: 全部；所有；完全
- Collocation: all day;above all;all over
- Example: All students must wear school uniforms.
- 例句: 所有学生都必须穿校服。
- Synonym: every;entire
- Antonym: none;some
- MemoryTip: all of us = 我们全部。

## 34. if

- CEFR: A1
- POS: conj.
- IPA (UK): /ɪf/
- Chinese: 如果；是否
- Collocation: if possible;what if;as if
- Example: If it rains, we will cancel the trip.
- 例句: 如果下雨，我们就取消这次旅行。
- Synonym: whether;when
- MemoryTip: 真实条件用 will；虚拟则改时态。

## 35. one

- CEFR: A1
- POS: num.; pron.; det.
- IPA (UK): /wʌn/
- Chinese: 一；一个人；某一个
- Collocation: one day;one of;the one
- Example: One of my friends lives abroad.
- 例句: 我的一个朋友住在国外。
- Synonym: a;single
- Antonym: many;none
- MemoryTip: 可替代前文名词：this one / that one。

## 36. would

- CEFR: A1
- POS: modal
- IPA (UK): /wʊd/
- Chinese: 会；愿意；过去将来
- Collocation: would like;would rather;would you
- Example: I would like a glass of water, please.
- 例句: 请给我一杯水。
- Synonym: could;might
- MemoryTip: would like = want 的更礼貌说法。

## 37. about

- CEFR: A1
- POS: prep.; adv.

- Chinese: 关于；大约；到处
- Collocation: talk about;about time;think about
- Example: We talked about our weekend plans after lunch.

- Synonym: regarding;around
- Antonym: exactly
- MemoryTip: about 可记成绕着一件事转。

## 38. can

- CEFR: A1
- POS: modal; noun
- IPA (UK): /kæn/
- Chinese: 能；可以；罐头
- Collocation: can do;as you can;can of
- Example: Can you open the window, please?
- 例句: 请你把窗户打开好吗？
- Synonym: could;may;able to
- Antonym: cannot
- MemoryTip: 否定 can't；过去/委婉用 could。

## 39. which

- CEFR: A1
- POS: pron.; det.
- IPA (UK): /wɪtʃ/
- Chinese: 哪一个；……的（关系代词）
- Collocation: which one;in which;of which
- Example: Which colour do you prefer?

- Synonym: that;what
- MemoryTip: 问选择用 which；非限定从句也常用。

- Antonym: here

## 41. know
