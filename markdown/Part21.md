## 1001. shoot

- CEFR: A2
- POS: verb; noun
- IPA (UK): /ʃuːt/
- Chinese: 射击；拍摄
- Collocation: shoot a film;shoot at;photo shoot
- Example: They will shoot the final scene of the film tomorrow.
- 例句: 他们明天将拍摄这部电影的最后一场戏。
