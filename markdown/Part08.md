## 351. remember

- CEFR: A1
- POS: verb
- IPA (UK): /rɪˈmembə(r)/
- Chinese: 记得；记住；纪念
- Collocation: remember to;remember doing;remember me to
- Example: Please remember to lock the door.
- 例句: 请记得锁门。
- Synonym: recall;recollect;keep in mind
- Antonym: forget
- MemoryTip: remember to do=记得去做；doing=记得做过。
