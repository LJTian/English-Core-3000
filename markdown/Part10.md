## 451. certain

- CEFR: A1
- POS: adj.; pron.
- IPA (UK): /ˈsɜːtn/
- Chinese: 确定的；某一；必然的
- Collocation: for certain;certain to;a certain
- Example: I am certain that we met before.
- 例句: 我确信我们以前见过。
- Synonym: sure;particular;definite
- Antonym: uncertain;doubtful
- MemoryTip: a certain + 名词 = 某个……。
